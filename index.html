Escape From Hueb

A Flash based game with NO VISUALS

Escape from Hueb is an experiment in audio interactivity. There are NO VISUALS. ONLY AUDIO. It is a fully interactive puzzle game.

What you need to play

  • HEADPHONES. You can try with speakers, but audio positioning will be much more difficult.
  • A Flash enabled browser and Flash player 10. Get it here
  • A mouse. Game is not yet playable by keyboard alone.
  • Monitor NOT required. In fact, try playing with your monitor off!
